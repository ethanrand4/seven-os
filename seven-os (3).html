Communication principles — Director level
1

Lead with outcome, not activity. "Completed competitive analysis — recommendation is X" not "spent time researching competitors."

2

Surface risks before they become problems. "Flagging a potential dependency on engineering resource in week 3" shows you're ahead of it.

3

Connect your work to the big objective — beat Super 6, scale across Entain. Every update should feel like it's in service of something larger.

4

End with what you need from them. Forward the conversation — don't just report, advance.
